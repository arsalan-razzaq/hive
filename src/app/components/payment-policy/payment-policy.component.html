Payment Policy

Nadia Yoshiokaprovides flexible and secure payment options to suit customer preferences. Customers can choose between using the **Moyasar** payment gateway for online payments or **Cash on Delivery (COD)** for in-person payment upon receiving their orders.

Moyasar Payment Gateway

For convenient and secure transactions, Nadia Yoshiokaintegrates the Moyasar payment gateway, allowing customers to complete payments online with confidence. Moyasar supports various payment methods, ensuring flexibility for all users.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Customers who prefer paying at the time of delivery can select the Cash on Delivery option. This option enables customers to inspect their orders upon arrival before making a payment.
